## Overview

Tile Mate ("the app") is a tile calculation and estimation tool for professional tilers. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

---

## Data We Collect

### Data You Enter
Tile Mate allows you to save:
- Project names and client details (name, phone, email, address)
- Tile presets (sizes and prices)
- Calculation history
- Company branding details (your business name, phone, email)

**All of this data is stored locally on your device only.** It is never transmitted to any server or third party by us.

### Data Collected Automatically
Tile Mate uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Device identifiers
- IP address
- Approximate location
- App usage data

This data is collected by Google and is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## How We Use Your Data

- **Project and calculation data** — used only to provide the app's features. Stored on your device.
- **Backup files** — when you use the Export Backup feature, a JSON file is saved to your device. You control where this file goes. We do not receive it.
- **Ad data** — used by Google AdMob to serve relevant advertisements.

---

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third party.

The only third-party data collection is through Google AdMob. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details.

---

## Data Storage and Security

All app data (projects, presets, history, branding) is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. We have no access to this data.

If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is deleted.

---

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete any project, preset, or history entry within the app at any time
- Export your data using the Backup feature in Settings
- Uninstall the app to delete all stored data

---

## Children's Privacy

Tile Mate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
